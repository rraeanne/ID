Our Fragrances

At Noir Soleil, fragrance is more than just a scent—it is an experience. Our carefully curated collection blends artistry with sustainability, offering refillable, eco-conscious perfumes designed to evolve with you. Whether you are drawn to bold, mysterious notes or soft, floral harmonies, our Essence Empire program allows you to indulge in luxury while reducing waste. Not sure where to start? Our personalized fragrance quiz will guide you to your perfect match.

ragrance tells a story—a symphony of carefully crafted notes that evoke emotion, memory, and allure.
