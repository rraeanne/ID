About Noir Solei

At Noir Solei, we believe in crafting perfumes that are not only a reflection of luxury and elegance but also a step towards sustainability. Our fragrances are more than just scents—they are an experience, designed to be cherished and relished by the individual. With our refillable perfume bottles, you can indulge in your favorite fragrances while contributing to a greener, more eco-conscious world.

The name Noir Solei translates to "Black Sun," a perfect representation of the harmonious blend of boldness and lightness that defines our creations. Each bottle is a symbol of elegance, crafted with the finest ingredients sourced from around the world.

Our refillable bottles are designed with sustainability in mind. By choosing to refill your perfume, you reduce waste and help us reduce our environmental footprint. We offer an exciting variety of scents, each thoughtfully created to evoke emotions and create lasting memories. From fresh florals to warm, musky tones, our scents are tailored to suit every personality and occasion.

At Noir Solei, fragrance is more than just a scent—it's a lifestyle. Our journey is just beginning, and we are dedicated to offering you a memorable and sustainable fragrance experience that stays with you long after you've left.

Why Choose Noir Soleil?

  • Sustainability: Refillable glass bottles, reducing waste and environmental impact.
  • Luxury Perfumes: Exquisite, high-quality fragrances from around the world.
  • Eco-conscious: A brand that cares about your fragrance experience and the planet.
  • Unique Scents: A carefully curated range of scents that suit every personality.
